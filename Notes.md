Our best configuration utilizes a 4-layer, 8-head GPT architecture scaled with an extended block size of 256 and an embedding size of 160 to maximize the representative history window. We applied weight tying between token embeddings and the language model head, allowing us to shift parameter budget toward contextual depth while staying safely under the hard 2,000,000 parameter limit. The attention projection layers were optimized by stripping out linear layer biases to minimize structural overhead. Training stability over the 2,000-step budget is maintained using an AdamW optimizer paired with a linear warmup and cosine annealing learning rate scheduler. Furthermore, deep residual paths are stabilized using a scaled initialization method proportional to the reciprocal square root of twice the layer depth.
